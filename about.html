Our Story

Education, Empowerment & Excellence

McZeal Academy is the flagship EdTech vertical of McZeal Global Services Pvt Ltd, an academic unit committed to exercising unique pracademic approaches with a blend of technology in the teaching-learning process.

We empower universities, academic institutions and individual learners to navigate the complexities and challenges of the ever-changing higher education domain. We bridge communities, capabilities, and communication to create avenues for learners to lay strong foundations towards sustainable development.

Headquartered in Nungambakkam, Chennai, McZeal Academy serves learners across India with world-class programmes that combine academic rigour with industry relevance.
